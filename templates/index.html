Senate Parliamentary Debate 2015

Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology

Senate Parliamentary Debate is the annual debating tournament of Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT), Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It thrives to provide a quality debating experience with unique motions.

Senate is an event under DA-IICT's annual techno-cultural festival Synapse, the second largest event of its kind in Gujarat. Synapse has witnessed some big celebrities and bands like Javed Ali, Indian Ocean, Nale, Devoid, Raghu Dixit and many more.
